# Burning Letter

A single-file HTML canvas animation of a handwritten letter catching fire and burning away.

## What it shows

An aged, yellowed sheet of paper covered in handwritten cursive rests on a dark wooden desk. After a brief pause, a flame ignites at the bottom-right corner and spreads organically across the page — the burn front advances with an irregular, flickering edge that never forms a straight line. Just ahead of the flames the paper scorches and browns, then chars black, then disappears entirely to reveal the desk beneath.

The fire is rendered with layered particles — a bright white-yellow core, an orange mid-flame, and translucent red tips that lick upward. Glowing embers detach from the burn edge and drift up on turbulent air, fading from orange to gray, while semi-transparent smoke rises and disperses above. A warm, flickering light spills onto the surrounding desk. The whole page is consumed in roughly 15 seconds, leaving only a few glowing ash fragments that slowly dim.

## Run it

Open `burning-letter.html` in any modern browser. No build step, no external libraries — it targets 60fps using only the Canvas API.
